About Campus Mobile

Empowering South African students and youth with affordable, reliable mobile data and connectivity.

Our Story

Campus Mobile was founded with a simple mission: to make mobile data accessible and affordable for every student and young person in South Africa. We believe that connectivity is a right, not a privilege, and we are committed to bridging the digital divide by offering flexible, low-cost data plans tailored to the needs of our community.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To empower the next generation with affordable, high-quality mobile data and seamless connectivity.

Our Vision

To be the leading mobile provider for students and youth, driving digital inclusion and opportunity across South Africa.

Our Values

Accessibility, Innovation, Community, Integrity, and Customer-Centricity.
