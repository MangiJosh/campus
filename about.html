About Campus Mobile

We're on a mission to make mobile data accessible and affordable for every South African student.

Our Story

Born from the need to bridge the digital divide in South African education.

Empowering Students Through Technology

Campus Mobile was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure that every South African student has access to affordable, reliable mobile data to support their educational journey.

We recognized that the high cost of mobile data was preventing many students from accessing essential online resources, participating in virtual classes, and staying connected with their educational communities.

By partnering with major universities and leveraging student-focused pricing models, we've created a mobile service that understands and addresses the unique needs of South African students.

Our Mission & Vision
