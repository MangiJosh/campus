Our Story

Campus Mobile was born from a simple yet powerful realization: every student deserves access to affordable connectivity. Founded by a team of young South African entrepreneurs who experienced firsthand the struggle of expensive data, we set out to revolutionize how students and youth access mobile services. We believe that connectivity is not just a convenience—it's a fundamental right that opens doors to education, opportunity, and limitless possibilities.
