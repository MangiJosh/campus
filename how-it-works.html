How It Works

Follow these simple steps to get your Campus Mobile SIM card and start saving on data.

1

Order Your SIM

Fill out our simple online form with your student details and delivery address. We'll deliver your SIM card to your campus.

2

Activate Your SIM

Insert your SIM card and follow the activation instructions sent via SMS. Your SIM will be ready to use within minutes.

3

Choose Your Plan

Select from our flexible data bundles - daily, weekly, or monthly plans. Pay only for what you need, when you need it.

4

Start Saving

Enjoy affordable data with zero-rated educational content and social media. Recharge anytime through our app or website.
