3 Simple Steps to Get Started

From ordering to activation, we've made the process as simple as possible for students.

1

Order Your SIM

Choose your preferred plan and order your Campus Mobile SIM card online. It's quick and easy!

  • Select from our student-friendly plans
  • Provide your student ID for discounts
  • Choose delivery or campus pickup
  • Free SIM card included

Activate & Setup

Once you receive your SIM, activation takes just a few minutes. No complicated setup required.

  • Insert SIM into your phone
  • Follow activation instructions
  • Download our mobile app
  • Start using immediately

Recharge & Enjoy

Top up your data whenever you need it. No contracts, no hidden fees, just flexible data when you want it.

  • Recharge via app or website
  • Multiple payment options
  • Instant activation
  • 24/7 support available
